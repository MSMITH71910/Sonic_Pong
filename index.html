PONG

Recreated by Michael R Smith

How to Play:

Goal: Score points by making the ball pass your opponent's paddle.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Mouse: Move horizontally to control your paddle
  • Keyboard: Use 'A' and 'D' keys to move left and right

Player 2 Controls:

  • Keyboard: Use 'Left Arrow' and 'Right Arrow' keys to move

Game Controls:

  • Press 'Esc' key or right-click to pause the game
  • First player to reach the winning score wins!
